OUR MISSION

As a specialized University we are committed to
the training of skilled and innovative work-force
that would transform Nigeria's natural resources
into goods and services driven by
entrepreneurship and Information and
Communication Technology (ICT), to positively
affect the economy and thus the quality of life of
her people

About FUTMinna

The Federal University of Technology, Minna is a Federal Government owned University in Nigeria. It was established on 1st February, 1983. Admin Login The objective for its establishment is to give effect to the Nation’s drive for the much-needed self-reliance in Science, Engineering and especially Technology.
